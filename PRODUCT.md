# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

OccUhealth Ireland's homepage is for HR leaders, business owners, senior managers, and Environmental Health & Safety decision-makers in Irish organisations. They arrive needing credible support for Occupational Health, Environmental Health & Safety, absence management, compliance, and workforce wellbeing.

## Product Purpose

The site presents OccUhealth Ireland as a trusted B2B healthcare and workplace risk partner. Success means visitors quickly understand the combined Occupational Health and EHS offer, recognise the company as established and clinically credible, and feel confident contacting the team.

## Brand Personality

Professional, clinical-human, established. The tone should feel calm, decisive, trustworthy, Irish, and practical rather than fashionable or promotional.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS landing pages, glassmorphism, startup gradients, futuristic health-tech styling, wellness influencer aesthetics, hospital cliches, cartoon illustration packs, excessive roundness, dark generic dashboards, and PowerPoint layouts copied directly onto the web.

## Design Principles

Lead with integrated expertise: make the connection between Occupational Health and Environmental Health & Safety obvious on the first visit.

Make people visible: use workplace and human-centred imagery so the site feels personal without becoming informal.

Translate the deck, do not paste it: preserve the blue, lime, white, large typography, and circular motif while creating a native web experience.

Use confidence over clutter: favour strong editorial compositions, selective data-free business case statements, and concise copy.

Keep the path to contact clear: every major section should make it easier for a decision-maker to understand the offer or speak to the team.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and interactive elements. Support reduced motion, keyboard navigation, readable mobile layouts, descriptive alt text, semantic HTML, and British or Irish English spelling.
